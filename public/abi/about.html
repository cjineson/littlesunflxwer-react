About Little Sunflxwer

Little Sunflxwer was created by Yasmin Ineson, the creator of all things cute and colourful, in 2020. Yasmin hand-crochets all of her products. Yasmin loves drawing. The official Little Sunflxwer website was made by Chris Ineson, Abigail Ineson and Kieran Hill. Abigail Ineson and Chris Ineson did the coding to make the website and Kieran Hill did the Design including the logo. This website is not the official website. It was made by Abigail Ineson.
